Our Project

The main objective of this project is to help the student to select the colleges for counselling after completing their 12th std. Due to covid, counselling process through online becomes very   difficult for some students. Even me and my friends faced this issue. After getting to know that this problem is faced by most of the people, so we took this problem to provide solution.Most of the students from rural areas
mostly rely on websites and social media for knowing about the colleges which sometimes may have wrong information or sometimes may mislead them to choose the college wrongly.
